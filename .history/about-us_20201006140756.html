Dial a teacher Mission

We know you have many options when it comes to choosing tutoring services for you or your child. At Dial a teacher, it is our mission to match you with the best tutor available, at an affordable rate, to maximize learning success.

In order to attract and keep the highest quality tutors, it is also our mission to take care of them, and pay them higher rates than many other tutoring services offer.

It’s our belief that when you need tutoring services, you deserve a professional.

Our History

Our most valuable asset is our team of tutors and institutes. We only work with experienced, certified, background-checked teachers and institute. This sets us apart from almost every other tutoring service out there.In addition,all of our tutoring sessions are both one-on-one and group. We added one on one (private tuition), because we feel that students who need a little extra help don’t need to sit in group class and compete for the teacher’s attention.We’re confident that our rates for tutoring are very competitive in the industry,and we are proud to be paying our teachers at a level their professionalism deserves.
